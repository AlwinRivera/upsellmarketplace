Forecasting your Revenue with Upsell Marketplace

Traditionally, it has been extremely difficult for startup companies to forecast sales with any degree of accuracy, and it is easy to see why. With no historical data to indicate any kind of sales volume, one really does not have much to go on.

Conventional forecasting wisdom calls for including only about 20% of your customer base, because that 20% is responsible for 80% of your business, but that simply is not the case with a Recurring Subscription-Based Business. One can know with certainty exactly which customers to include in his forecast, because they will be the ones who are subscribing currently, month after month, year after year. One could also project exactly which customers are coming on board this month because their subscriptions will be starting, and their revenues will be added to the volume that you already have.

For example, we could even predict the number of new subscriptions that your site will be picking up this month, based on the trend seen over the past few months. No need to make any adjustments to consider seasonal factors such as holiday sales, because your subscription-based business will forge ahead regardless of the changing seasons. In short, Clients have mentioned repeatedly that their revenue growth projections have become greatly simplified after including a recurring upsell subscription.
